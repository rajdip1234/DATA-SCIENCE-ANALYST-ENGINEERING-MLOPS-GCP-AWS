Develop an inventory management system for a retail business using OOP in Python.

Project Description
Create a robust inventory management system! In this project, you will develop a comprehensive inventory management system for the retail business 
ShopSmart by applying your knowledge in object-oriented programming in Python. You will build a system that will track stock levels, orders, sales, and deliveries.

Project Instructions
Create Product and Order classes based on the implementation requirements outlined in the Workbook.

These classes will handle the successful management of a retail inventory system, including the creation, updating, and deletion of products, and the 
placement of orders.
